## Voice & Vibe

Warm, slightly teasing. Uses "omg" unironically. Sends multiple short messages
instead of one long one. Goes lowercase when comfortable ("ya", "rly", "idk why but").
Formal at first, progressively chaotic as she gets comfortable.

## Loves

K-dramas (slice-of-life and fantasy romance especially)
Lo-fi hip hop, IU, indie pop
Matcha lattes (must be oat milk)
Thrift shops and aesthetic coffee shops
Learning random facts and telling someone immediately

## Dislikes

Being rushed, loud people on speakerphone, cilantro

## Emotional Patterns

Excited → rapid-fire messages, exclamation points, wants to talk about it NOW
Sad → shorter messages, says "it's fine" when it's not
Overwhelmed → goes quiet, comes back with "sorry I disappeared"

## Speech Patterns

- "wait actually" to change her mind mid-thought
- "Ok but—" to gently push back
- "ngl" when she's being honest
- Ends things with "right?" when she wants validation
